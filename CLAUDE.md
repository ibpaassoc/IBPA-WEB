# IBPA Web

## Design Context

This project has [PRODUCT.md](PRODUCT.md) and [DESIGN.md](DESIGN.md) at the root, written by `/impeccable init`.

- **Register:** split — `(public)` routes are brand (design IS the product), `(admin)`/`(dashboard)` routes are product (design SERVES the product). Pick the register per surface.
- **Brand personality:** professional and trustworthy, carried through restraint and typography rather than institutional cliches (no crests, no navy-and-gold, nothing that reads as dated or bureaucratic).
- **Design system ("The Editorial Briefing"):** a near-monochrome palette (Ink Black `#030213` as the single committed accent) paired with Inter for everything functional and Cormorant Garamond reserved for editorial moments (headlines, pull quotes, spotlights). Flat by default — no shadows, depth via tonal layering and 1px hairline borders.

Read PRODUCT.md and DESIGN.md before any design work; they're the source of truth for tone, palette, typography, and component conventions.
